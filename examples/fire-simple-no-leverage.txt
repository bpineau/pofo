# FIRE Simple (no leverage, no trend) -- the explainable-to-anyone build
# Idea: the control group for the fire-* family: what a prudent decumulation
#   looks like with ONLY plain, unleveraged, self-explanatory bricks: world
#   equity, euro government bonds, short euro linkers, gold, cash. No
#   futures overlay, no managed futures, nothing that needs a manager or a
#   model to keep working. The price of that simplicity is a weaker answer
#   to stagflation (nominal bonds + equities can fall together in real
#   terms, 2022-style) -- which is exactly what the trend and efficient-core
#   builds are trying to fix. Compare and decide what complexity buys.

#   (global history supports ~2.5-3% withdrawals, not 4%).
