# Stagflation Bunker -- a sleeve for the regime nobody covers
# Idea: persistent inflation with weak growth is the regime that defeats
#   both classic 60/40s and efficient-core builds (equities and NOMINAL
#   bonds fall together in real terms; 2022 was the trailer). This sleeve
#   holds only the assets research finds actually work there: trend (the
#   most reliable, mainly via its commodity channel), broad commodities
#   held directly, gold, and short linkers so the defensive leg cannot be
#   inflated away. Not a standalone portfolio: a lab for the sleeve that
#   the fire-* builds embed at ~30%, and a stress-test companion (compare
#   it to your core over 2021-2022).
# Inspired by: Neville, Draaisma, Funnell, Harvey & van Hemert, "The Best
#   Strategies for Inflationary Times" (2021).
