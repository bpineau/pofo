# Miller 50/40/10 -- the paper that put trend into retirement portfolios
# Idea: Andrew Miller's 2017 result: replacing 10 points of a 60/40 with
#   trend-following managed futures (50% stocks / 40% bonds / 10% trend)
#   materially raises the withdrawal rate a retiree can sustain, because
#   trend earns its keep in exactly the long bear markets that kill
#   withdrawal plans (2000-02, 2008, 2022). The simplest evidence-based
#   upgrade to the classic retirement 60/40.
# Inspired by: Miller, "Using Trend-Following Managed Futures to Increase
#   Expected Withdrawal Rates" (SSRN 3048332, 2017).
# Drawn at the classic 4% here, on purpose: compare its money path with
#   tradi-60-40 plus '#meta withdraw' to see the trend leg pay.
